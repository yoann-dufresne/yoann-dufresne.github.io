My name is Yoann Dufresne. I am a computer scientist holding a PhD in bioinformatics from the university of Lille (France). I do my research in the Bonsai Group on exact algorithmic for nonribosomal peptide annotations.

During my 3 years of thesis, I gave many lectures in the computer science department of Lille 1, from programming basics in C or JAVA for bahelor’s degree students to Advance algorithms and Kernel programming for master’s degree students.

I’m also a frequent participant of algorithms contests and soon I will add a blog on this website to explain my reflexions on different algorithm problems.
